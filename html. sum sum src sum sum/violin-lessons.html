My Experience with Music

I started my music journey six years ago with the viola. Since then, I've had the privilege of performing in a variety of ensembles. I have proudly participated in both SCMEA (Suffolk County Music Educators' Association) festivals, earning 3rd chair, and LISFA (Long Island String Festival Association), achieving first chair and leading the viola section in my first year of participating in these festivals. Additionally, this year marks my 4th year participating in New York State School Music Association (NYSSMA) solos, where I attained a score of 99 on my most recent Level 5 solo. This year, I am preparing to perform a level six solo. I continue to participate in these special festivals throughout my music career. I picked up the violin over a year ago and now play the violin in my school's chamber orchestra. I am also a proud member of the Lindenhurst Marching Band since the spring of 2024, where I play the Baritone. This experience has built a foundation of understanding for wind instruments. Building on this foundation, I learned trombone and currently perform with my school's band. In my private practice, I enjoy playing the alto saxophone and am currently in the process of learning the trumpet and clarinet. Through these diverse musical experiences, I continue to develop my skills and foster my love for music.
